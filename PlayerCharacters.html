Histories of Saltmarsh Region

Urgor Goliath Fighter (Battlemaster)

Urgor is searching for an artefact of his tribe that was once owned by Arkon Demoncarver. Arkon united the Goliath tribes for a time until he left and was lost.

Belizar Half-Elf Rogue (Swashbuckler)

Belizar comes from the Wild coast and was taken there as a child by Namaneed. His mother was killed and his father was recently found out to be Eda Oweland. A checkered past of smuggling, he is searching for a place to call home.

Seja Halfing Life Cleric/Celestial Warlock

After Seja's family was killed by dire wolves from Dreadwood, he joined a monastery and became a priest of Yondalla. She's given him a vision of the downfall of Saltmarsh and a quest to save the halfings that left Heatherholm.

Nine Lives More Tabaxi Wizard (Bladesinging)

Nine lines served on several ships (saving the crew of one) before arriving in Saltmarsh. She spent some time with elves learning `bladesinging'. She's very curious about anything interesting, less so with the rest...
